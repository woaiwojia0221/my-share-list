Hebei Dezny Chemical Technology Co., Ltd.
 is a chemical company integrating cellulose product
 research and development, production, 
sales and related services. 
The company's development focuses on the cellulose ether industry,
 focusing on hydroxypropyl methylcellulose(HPMC), 
methylhydroxyethylcellulose (MHEC),
redispersible latex powder (VAE), 
hydroxyethylcellulose (HEC) 
and Research and development, 
manufacturing and sales of functional cellulose ethers.
 A complete system featuring scientific research and development, 
large-scale production, application technology industry chain extension
 and technical services has been formed. 
Dezny: Production capacity 20,000T/year, 
hydroxypropyl methylcellulose 10,000T/year, 
methyl hydroxyethyl cellulose 4,000T/year, 
redispersible latex powder 2,000T/year, 
hydroxyethyl cellulose 2,000T/year; 
products Widely used in: water-based paints, 
daily chemicals, washing, putty powder, tile adhesive, 
mortar and other fields. 
Dezny attaches great importance to product research 
and development and customer needs, 
establishing R&D centers and application laboratories, 
and provides customized production and flexible services 
through in-depth docking with customers. 
According to the continuous development of market demand, 
Dezny continues to develop new products, explore new fields 
and serve new markets all year round. Products are exported to 
more than 20 countries. Dezny is committed to becoming a 
high-quality supplier in the field of cellulose ethers.
